✅ Responsible Social Media Use Checklist for College Students

✔️ Do’s
•	Think before you post – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate. 
•	Protect your privacy – Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings. 
•	Verify information before sharing – Avoid spreading fake news or rumors. 
•	Maintain a positive digital footprint – Share content that reflects your values and goals. 
•	Respect others online – Be polite and mindful in comments and messages. 
❌ Don’ts
•	Don’t share personal or sensitive information – Avoid posting addresses, phone numbers, etc. 
•	Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling – Never harass or insult others online. 
•	Don’t overshare – Keep a balance between online and offline life. 
•	Don’t click suspicious links – Protect yourself from scams and malware. 
•	Don’t post when emotional – Avoid posting in anger or frustration to prevent regret later.
