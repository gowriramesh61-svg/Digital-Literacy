Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)

General Safety Tips
Use strong, unique passwords for each account (combine letters, numbers, symbols).
Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on email, banking, and social media accounts.
Verify links before clicking – check the URL carefully, especially for scholarships, internships, or login pages.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts.
Limit personal information sharing (phone number, address, college ID) on social media profiles.
Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store
 or Apple App Store
Log out from shared devices in labs, libraries, or cyber cafés.
Be cautious of “too good to be true” offers (fake internships, giveaways, or job scams).

Financial / UPI Safety Tips
Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank.
Always double-check the receiver’s UPI ID or QR code before making payments.
Do not accept “collect requests” from unknown people on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.
Turn on transaction alerts and regularly check bank statements to detect unauthorized activity early.
If Something Goes Wrong
Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (especially for financial fraud—quick action can help recover money)
